The Course

The complete course consists of 12.5 hours tuition
(mother & birth partner)

  • Courses are taught on a private, one-to-one basis
  • Classes are held in Isleworth or in your own home, according to individual requirements
  • Couples are taught relaxation, breathing, self-hypnosis and massage techniques to use during labour and birth
  • The classes offer couples the opportunity to prepare an individualized birth plan with myself in preparation for their birth

Included in the cost:

  • 5 × 2 ½ hour HypnoBirthing® sessions for mum and Birth Companion
  • The HypnoBirthing® Book by Marie Mongan
  • HypnoBirthing® relaxation MP3
  • All course handouts & materials
  • Birth preferences tailored to individual needs
  • Contact with myself via email/telephone for the duration of the course

Fees:

Private Course: £300 per couple

Optional HypnoMothering Session:

  • 1 × 2 ½ hour HypnoMothering® session for mum
  • HypnoMothering® audio downloads

Additional £80 per session

Venue

Classes can either take place in my Home Office located in Isleworth or privately in your own home. The home office is a quiet space designed to deliver HypnoBirthing® classes. A calm setting to help you learn and prepare for the birth of your baby.

Isleworth is within 20 minutes of both Richmond and Twickenham.
